ABOUT US

We Provide The Best
Service To Build

Fabricon Integrated Services Nig. Ltd is a fully indigenous fabrication company incorporated in Nigeria in 2024. We specialize in onshore and offshore fabrication, design, and installation of steel structures, pipelines, tanks, and platforms. Committed to innovation, safety, and quality, we deliver tailored solutions across construction, maintenance, and infrastructure projects. Our goal is to provide exceptional value while fostering strong client and community relationships.
